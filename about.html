PEOPLE. CULTURE. DIVERSITY & AESTHETICS


Pine City Mall is an iconic destination located in a beautiful small town called Matatiele, in the Eastern Cape, South Africa. Pine City Mall offers convenience and variety and welcomes millions of people from all over the country and the rest of the world. Pine City Mall is a symbol of diversity and unity, where people from all walks of life can work, play, dine, shop, find pleasure and just be themselves while enjoying the vibrant spirit and authentic local culture that exist in the vicinity.

Pine City has grown from being just a street full of vendors to this amazing neighbourhood full of life and oportunities. It is a place where people come together to live, work, connect and take a break from their normal lives. Pine City Mall creates job opportunites for people around the world whether young, old, black or white because we believe that a person should not be excluded because of their age, race or nationality.
